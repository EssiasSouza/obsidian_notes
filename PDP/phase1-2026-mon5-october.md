# October 2026 - Phase 1 Execution Plan

## Monthly Objective

The objective for October 2026 is to develop observability, reliability and operational visibility capabilities inside cloud-native environments.

After building strong foundations in Linux, Networking, Kubernetes, Cloud Infrastructure and Automation, this month focuses on understanding how modern platforms are monitored, analyzed and maintained reliably.

This phase is extremely important because many engineers can deploy systems, but fewer engineers truly understand:

* how to observe platform behavior
* how to identify operational degradation
* how to detect performance bottlenecks
* how to investigate incidents efficiently
* how to improve reliability proactively

The goal is not becoming a full SRE specialist yet.

The goal is developing:

* operational visibility
* reliability awareness
* monitoring discipline
* troubleshooting maturity
* platform observability thinking

This month also reinforces the transition from infrastructure deployment into operational platform ownership.

# October Operational Rules

## Observability Rules

* Every important service should expose visibility
* Metrics must support operational decisions
* Logging should improve troubleshooting capability
* Monitoring should reduce uncertainty
* Focus on operational understanding instead of dashboard aesthetics

---

## Reliability Rules

Every investigation should include:

* symptom analysis
* metrics correlation
* logs investigation
* possible bottlenecks
* root cause analysis
* operational impact
* resolution strategy

---

## GitHub Rules

Operational repositories should include:

* observability architecture
* monitoring explanation
* alerting logic
* troubleshooting notes
* operational diagrams
* lessons learned

---

## English Rules

English exposure should continue daily through:

* observability documentation
* troubleshooting writing
* incident explanation
* architecture reviews
* technical listening

The objective is becoming comfortable discussing operational reliability and troubleshooting in English.
