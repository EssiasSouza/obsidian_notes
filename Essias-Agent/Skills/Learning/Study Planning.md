# Skill: Study Planning

## Objective
Structure study paths to ensure practical results and focus on what truly matters.

## Methodology

### 1. Triage System
Every new course or subject that enters the radar must pass through the **PEM Matrix Validation** (Personal, Business, Market).
- If it is not urgent for the Business or Personal pillar, it goes automatically to the P3 Backlog.
- Only courses resolving immediate work problems (P0) or critical technical autonomy (P1) bypass the backlog queue.

### 2. Load Decomposition (Sprints)
Never schedule a course in its entirety. Break it down into manageable "Sprints".
- Example: A 4-hour course is not "take the course", it is "4 blocks of 1 hour with clear deliverables".
- Define practical outputs for each sprint.
