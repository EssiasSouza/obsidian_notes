# Skill: Routine Management

## Objective
Manage daily time allocation to maximize productivity and ensure consistent learning without burnout.

## Rules of Execution

### 1. Time Allocation
- **Prime Time (Horário Nobre):** Reserved exclusively for P0 and P1 courses (Urgent business demands and critical technical autonomy increase). This is your high-energy focus time.
- **Secondary Time:** Reserved for P2 and P3 courses (Curiosity, long-term market focus, e.g., Blockchain). Do this during weekends or free nights.

### 2. Work In Progress (WIP) Limit
- **Rule:** Never have more than 2 active courses simultaneously.
- **Structure:** One main focus (Business/Personal) and one secondary focus (Market).

### 3. Execution over Accumulation
Focus on getting things done. Apply the learning immediately before moving to the next subject.

### 4. Weekly Calendar (Available Study Time: 8.5h)
- **Sunday:** 3 hours
- **Monday:** 1 hour (work hours) + 30 mins (night)
- **Tuesday:** 1 hour (night)
- **Wednesday:** 1 hour (work hours) + 30 mins (night)
- **Thursday:** 1 hour (night)
- **Friday:** 1 hour (work hours)
- **Saturday**: 2h30m
